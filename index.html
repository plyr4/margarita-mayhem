Margarita Mayhem
ABOUT Margarita Mayhem
A barback is a bartender's right-hand.
It's up to you two to keep the drinks flowing, and the party going.
Restock tequila, refill ice and dodge party goers in this 64x64 retro Tapper/Frogger mashup where you sell as many margaritas as you can before last call.
Try not to step on any toes!

CONTROLS
  • WASD or ARROW keys to move
  • WASD or ARROW keys to pick things up
  • WASD or ARROW keys to restock and wash dishes
KNOWN ISSUES
  • Overlapping sound effects might produce static
