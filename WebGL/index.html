Margarita Mayhem
ABOUT Margarita Mayhem
Take control of a barback, a bartender's right-hand.
It's up to you two to keep the drinks flowing, and the party going!
Restock tequila, refill ice, and hit the dance floor in this 64x64 retro Tapper/Frogger mashup!
Don't step on any toes!

CONTROLS
  • WASD/ARROW keys to control your character
  • WASD/ARROW keys to pick things up
  • WASD/ARROW keys to put things down
